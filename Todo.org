* RESEARCH
** Spatial Hashing
   Instead of a fixed grid array, use a hash map keyed by grid cell.
   - Advantages:
     - Works for huge worlds
     - Only stores occupied cells
     - Cheap insertion/removal
   - Often used in:
     - sandbox games
     - procedural worlds
     - ECS engines
